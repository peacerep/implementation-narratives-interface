Latest News/Facts Matter 

The security situation along the contact line in eastern Ukraine has seriously deteriorated over the last two weeks.



Fighting was concentrated in five main hotspots:

the area located between Avdiivka, Yasynuvata and Donetsk airport;

the area Novozvanivka, Kalynove, Veselohorivka and Troitske;

the area located south-east and east of Svitlodarsk;

the eastern and north-eastern sector of Mariupol, and the western outskirts of Horlivka.

located between 

Weapons proscribed by the Minsk Agreements continued to be used in high numbers.

The SMM registered a sharp increase in the use of tanks, artillery and multiple launch rocket systems.



The sides continued to endanger civilians by shelling populated areas as well as areas close to crossing routes.

Civilian lives were also jeopardized by the positioning of weapons and hardware close to residential and civilian infrastructure.



On 9 November shelling occurred close to an SMM patrol team near Verkhnioshyrokivske.
