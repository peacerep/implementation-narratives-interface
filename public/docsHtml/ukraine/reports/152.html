LATEST NEWS 

The SMM observed a general de-escalation starting from 15 February.

Ceasefire violations have been recorded at:

Bakhmutka, Debaltseve, Donetsk Airport, Pervomaisk, Shyrokyne, Trokhizbenka, Vrubivka.



On 24 February the SMM Chief Monitor Ambassador Ertugrul Apakan and Deputy Chief Monitor Alexander Hug participated in a meeting in Paris of the Foreign Ministers of the Normandy format.



On 26 February Hug took part in a symposium “After Flight MH17:

Reparation & Repercussion” in Utrecht.

On 27 February Hug participated in a roundtable dis- cussion in Berlin on “Weapons, Sanctions or Diplomacy - Ways to De-escalate the Conflict in Eastern Ukraine”.



On 27 February Ambassador Apakan addressed the UN Security Council on the situation in Ukraine.



On 6 March Ambassador Apakan took part in the first meeting of “the oversight mechanism” in the Normandy format, which was envisaged as part of the Minsk Package of Measures of 12 February.



On 9 March the SMM facilitated the demining of the village of Kominternove by the Ukrainian Armed Forces.
