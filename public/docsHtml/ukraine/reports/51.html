The SMM continued to monitor and verify withdrawal of weaponry as foreseen in the Addendum to the “Package of measures”.

The SMM continued to observe a relatively calm situation in the east. Most smallarms fire and explosions recorded by the SMM were attributed to training or demining activities. However the SMM recorded several incidents where shelling allegedly caused casualties and damage.

On 11 October the SMM issued a Spot Report “Shelling at and around Donetsk airport”: www.osce.org/ukraine-smm/191421

The Chief Monitor of the OSCE Special Monitoring Mission to Ukraine, Ambassador Ertugrul Apakan, voiced concern over this incident in Donetsk city.

In order to generally enhance its efficiency in patrolling the SMM has established Forward Patrol Bases in government-controlled Novoaidar and “LPR”-controlled Stakhanov in Luhansk region and in government-controlled Volnovakha in Donetsk region.

On 22 October the SMM Deputy Chief Monitor Alexander Hug will visit Donetsk region in order to assess the general security situation in the region and meet with the SMM teams. Hug will also meet with interlocutors to solicit support for opening additional SMM Forward Patrol Bases in the Donetsk region.
