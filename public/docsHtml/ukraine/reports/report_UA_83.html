Latest News/Facts Matter  The SMM continued to monitor and verify withdrawal of weaponry as foreseen in the Addendum to the “Package of measures.” 

The ceasefire in eastern Ukraine has been largely holding for almost two months now.

The majority of explosions have been attributed to demining and most firing incidents to training activities.

Nonetheless, there were a number of serious incidents mostly at and around the Donetsk airport that could not be attributed to such activities, some involving fatalities.

 On 22-26 October OSCE SMM Deputy Chief Monitor Alexander Hug visited Donetsk region.

He and the SMM team there met with internally displaced persons from Pisky.

Moreover, for the first time in the past two and half months, the SMM was able to access “DPR“-controlled Debaltseve, where local residents appeared to be maintaining a very basic existence.

Hug also met with interlocutors to solicit support for opening additional SMM Forward Patrol Bases and evaluated several potential locations for these bases in Donetsk region.

 On 27 October the SMM facilitated opening of the bridge at Stanytsia Luhanska on the contact line.

The opening was preceded by the SMM facilitating and monitoring the removal of unexploded ordnances on and around the bridge.
