On 5 August 2015 the SMM Chief Monitor Ambassador Ertugrul Apakan and the Deputy Chief Monitor Alexander Hug returned from the seventh round of negotiations of the Security Working Group within the Trilateral Contact Group in Minsk.

The sides discussed the plan for the withdrawal of tanks and smaller caliber weapons for two days.



The SMM has continued to observe ceasefire violations, including use of heavy weapons, tanks and smaller caliber weapons.



Most of the violence SMM observed occurred in residential areas.

Military positions in residential areas continued to endanger civilian lives.



The SMM continued to facilitate repair works on a major water pipeline between Maiorsk and Horlivka as well as in Spartak.

Several times the works were however interrupted by shelling.

Repair works in Spartak were completely finished on 11 August.



On 9 August, four OSCE armoured vehicles were destroyed by fire at SMM Donetsk residence.

SMM monitors were not injured.

On 9 August a peaceful demonstration took place outside the SMM office in Luhansk city.

Participants, who arrived together and seemed to have been co-ordinated by a number of people using radios, accused the SMM of what they alleged was a lack of objectivity.
