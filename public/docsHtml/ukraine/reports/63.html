The security situation in eastern Ukraine continued to be volatile and tense. Although the SMM recorded an increase in the number of ceasefire violations during the week of 9-15 January, the mission observed a decline in violations at all main hotspots the following week.

The vast majority of recorded ceasefire violations occurred to the east and south-east of Svitlodarsk, the Avdiivka-Yasynuvata-Donetsk airport area, to the north-east and east of Mariupol, in areas west and north of Horlivka, and in western Luhansk region. Stanytsia Luhanska, which was previously relatively quiet, saw a rise in violence in the second week, accounting for five per cent of all recorded ceasefire violations.

The disengagement process remained stalled.
