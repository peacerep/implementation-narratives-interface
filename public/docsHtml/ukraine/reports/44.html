LATEST NEWS 

The Special Monitoring Mission (SMM) recorded a lower number of ceasefire violations in both Donetsk and Luhansk regions between 20 April and 11 May compared to the previous reporting period, particularly since the beginning of May.



Monitors continued to observe heavy weapons in use and present in the security zone in violation of withdrawal lines and recorded significant number of weapons as missing from holding areas and permanent storage sites.



The Mission observed the presence of numerous weapons in violation of the respective withdrawal lines on 9 May parades in Donetsk and Luhansk cities.



The SMM faced further restrictions to its freedom of movement, particularly in areas not controlled by the Government.

The SMM was restricted from monitoring the border in “LPR”-controlled areas on a number of occasions.



Four civilians were killed close to a “DPR” checkpoint near Olenivka when shelling occurred in the early hours of 27 April.
