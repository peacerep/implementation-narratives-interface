Latest News/Facts Matter 

The SMM has noted numerous ceasefire violations in Donetsk region, particularly in the areas around Donetsk airport.

On 24 November alone the SMM registered over 570 explosions only in the Donetsk region.

The security situation in Luhansk region has also been volatile. 

The SMM registered ceasefire violations on both sides of the contact line involving weapons ranging from small arms to heavy artillery including weapons that should have been withdrawn according to the Minsk Addendum.



On 19 November, the OSCE SMM facilitated the retrieval of a Ukrainian Armed Forces soldier’s body near Zolote, Luhansk region.

Starting from 20 November the SMM followed up on the consequences of the explosions that damaged power supply pylons and the disruption of power supply from Kherson region to Crimea.
