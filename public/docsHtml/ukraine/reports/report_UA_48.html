Latest News/Facts Matter 

Despite claims by all sides of heavy weapons withdrawal, the SMM observed on a daily basis the presence and/or use of such weapons (e.g. main battle tanks (T-72, T-64), howitzers, anti-aircraft systems (Strela-10 120mm). 

On 10 May an SMM patrol came under machine-gun fire near the government-controlled village of Krymske. 

On 14-15 May SMM Chief Monitor Ambassador Apakan visited Eastern Ukraine.

This was his second trip to Kramatorsk and Sievierodonetsk.

Apakan met with representatives of minority communities, visited a centre for internally displaced people (IDP), met with the governor of Donetsk Oblast, Oleksandr Kikhtenko and paid a visit to SMM monitoring hubs in Kramatorsk and Sievierodonetsk. 

On 14 May he also attended the "East Ukraine Forum - Reconstruction Through Dialogue" organized by the OSCE Project Coordinator in Ukraine. 

The SMM issued its fifth thematic report “Protection of Civilians and their Freedom of Movement in the Donetsk and Luhansk Regions”.

Read the report in three languages (English, Ukrainian, Russian) here:

www.osce.org/ukraine-smm/156791 
