In keeping with the regular pattern of ebbs and flows of violence, the OSCE Special Monitoring Mission to Ukraine (SMM) observed a generally high number of ceasefire violations – mostly around Donetsk airport, the Yasynuvata-Avdiivka area, and on the Svitlodarsk-Debaltseve road. There have been occasional lulls and a notable decrease in the violence observed throughout Donbas from 18 to 21 June.

The SMM did, however, note a number of breaches of the ceasefire in areas that had been otherwise relatively calm, such as western Luhansk region and the western outskirts of Horlivka, Berezove and Volnovakha in Donetsk region.

The SMM is closely monitoring a tense situation at Stanytsia Luhanska bridge, where armed “LPR” members moved their positions forward across the contact line on 12 June, and the Ukrainian Armed Forces moved theirs forward three days later.

The SMM observed numerous weapons missing from storage sites and the presence of weapons in violation of withdrawal lines.

Freedom of movement of SMM monitors continued to be restricted, particularly in areas not controlled by the government.
