Latest News 

The SMM continued to facilitate repair works on a major water pipeline between Maiorsk and Horlivka.



On 17 July OSCE SMM Chief Monitor Ertugrul Apakan led a minute of silence in honour of the victims of flight MH17, downed over Ukraine one year ago.

SMM staff in Kyiv as well as monitors at the crash site in Hrabove participated. 

On 18 July the SMM observed shelling in Donetsk city centre for the first time in several months, as well as its aftermath in residential neighbourhoods.



On 21 July the SMM observed the planned movement of military equipment by both Ukrainian Armed Forces and “Donetsk People’s Republic”.



On 21 July in Minsk, the SMM Chief Monitor Ertugrul Apakan and Deputy Chief Monitor Alexander Hug participated in the 6th meeting of the Security Working Group under the auspices of the Trilateral Contact Group;

a preliminary agreement was reached on the withdrawal of tanks and smaller calibre weapons.



On 23-25 July Mr. Hug visited Donetsk to support the team after a protest outside the SMM location in Donetsk;

and to follow-up on the shelling of Donetsk city centre of 18 July, meeting with interlocutors and media.
