Latest News/Facts Matter  The security situation along the contact line in eastern Ukraine has seriously deteriorated with the SMM registering over 1,000 ceasefire violations daily with recent spikes over 2,000.

Shelling was concentrated in the traditional hotspots of the Donetsk airport-Yasynuvata-Avdiivka area, west and north of Horlivka, on the Debaltseve-Svitlodarsk road, areas east and north-east of Mariupol, and in western Luhansk region.

 Weapons proscribed by the Minsk agreements continued to be used in high numbers and the SMM registered an increase in the use of mortars, artillery and multiple launch rocket systems.

The SMM continued to observe weapons missing from storage sites as well as the presence of weapons in violation of withdrawal lines.

 The sides continued to endanger civilians by shelling populated areas as well as areas close to crossing routes.

Civilian lives were also jeopardized by the positioning of weapons and hardware close to residential and civilian infrastructure.

Consequently the SMM observed an increase in civilian casualties following a reduction in early September.  The SMM continued to monitor the disengagement process in Stanytsia Luhanska, Zolote and Petrivske, although its access remained restricted in all three areas.

 On 27 October a SMM vehicle was struck by a bullet near the contact line south-west of Donetsk city.
