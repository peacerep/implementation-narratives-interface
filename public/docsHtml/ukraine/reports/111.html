The Chief Monitor Ambassador Ertugrul Apakan visited Kramatorsk and Severodonetsk on 21 and 22 November. He met with the Luhansk Regional Governor.

The SMM facilitated a temporary ceasefire in Trokhizbenka in order for humanitarian aid to be delivered for local residents and to allow engineers in to make repairs on water and electricity supplies.

The SMM DCM Alexander Hug took part in panel discussion “Is There Anything Left of the Minsk Agreements” in the European Parliament in Strasbourg.

A rocket-propelled grenade and multiple anti-aircraft rounds were fired at an SMM patrol on 26 November.

The mission continues to expand. The total number of Seconded Monitors is 341 with 158 Monitors in Donetsk and Luhansk regions.
