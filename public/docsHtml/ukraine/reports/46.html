The SMM continued to monitor the disengagement areas near Stanytsia Luhanska, Zolote and Petrivske, and observed people inside the latter two areas. It also recorded 18 ceasefire violations inside the disengagement area near Petrivske.

Over the past two weeks, the SMM corroborated four civilian casualties, all inju- ries due to explosive objects. This brings the total number of civilian casualties that occurred since the beginning of 2021 to 37 (11 fatalities and 26 injuries). The majority of the casualties (27) were due to mines, unexploded ordnance and oth- er explosive objects, six were due to small-arms fire and four due to shelling.

During the reporting period, the Mission continued to facilitate and monitor ad- herence to localized ceasefires to enable maintenance and operation of critical civilian infrastructure, including maintenance to the phenol sludge reservoir near Novhorodske, Donetsk region, as well as water infrastructure near Krasnyi Lyman, Raivka and Pankivka benefiting around 250,000 civilians. Since the begin- ning of 2021, the SMM has facilitated repairs and maintenance to and operation of 58 civilian infrastructure objects (29 in Donetsk region and 29 in Luhansk re- gion) on both sides of the contact line, providing over six million civilians with water, electricity and gas.

The SMM monitored various areas in east and south-east Kherson region and along the coasts of the Black Sea and the Sea of Azov. It observed no change in the security situation.
