Between 20 April and 3 May, the SMM recorded about 9,800 ceasefire viola- tions, including about 2,750 explosions. This compares with about 7,200 and about 1,550, respectively, in the previous two-week period.

The Mission continued monitoring the security situation in and around the three disengagement areas, observing a calm situation inside the one near Stanytsia Luhanska, continued ceasefire violations (almost 200) inside the one near Petrivske, and the presence of persons inside the ones near Zolote and Petrivske.

The Mission corroborated reports of six civilian casualties, including one fatality, bringing the total since the beginning of 2020 to 35 (six fatalities and 29 injuries).

The SMM continued to facilitate and monitor adherence to localized ceasefires to enable repairs to, and construction and maintenance of, critical civilian infra- structure on both sides of the contact line. This included repairs to water infra- structure, upon which over 100,000 people depend, near Pankivka, Holmivskyi, Krasnyi Lyman, Zolote-5/Mykhailivka, Kamianka and Dolomitne.

Members of the armed formations continued to deny the SMM passage at checkpoints along official crossing routes in Donetsk and Luhansk regions (see Spot Report 17/2020, Spot Report 18/2020, Spot Report 19/2020 and Spot Re- port 20/2020).
