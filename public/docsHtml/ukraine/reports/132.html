Over the last two weeks, the SMM noted a decrease in the number of ceasefire violations recorded.

Following the announcement of the 25 August recommitment to the cease-fire, this number dropped to 47 violations on 26 August.

The level of violence decreased by 45% in the week from 28 August to 3 September as compared with the week before.



The use of high-calibre weapons fell dramatically between 28 August and 3 September, with three cases recorded – this is the lowest number recorded in a week since the Addendum to the Package of Measures came into force at the beginning of October 2015.

The Mission registered also fewer civilian casualties.

Between 28 August and 3 September, the SMM confirmed one case of injury to civilians (as opposed to one fatality and four injuries the week before).



The OSCE SMM monitors continued to contribute to the improvement of the humanitarian situation.

The Mission facilitated and monitored adherence to local ceasefires, co-ordinated by the Joint Centre for Control and Co-ordination, to enable repairs to the power plant in Shchastia, the Petrivske water pumping station near Artema, the Karbonit water pumping station near Zolote, a water pipeline in Pivdenne, the Vesela Hora-Khrystove power transmission Krasnyi Lyman.

In the past two weeks, from 21 August to 3 September, the SMM conducted 28 patrols on both sides of the contact line to support such activities.

The OSCE monitors’ freedom of movement was restricted due to the presence of mines in the disengagement areas and elsewhere.

Repeatedly, the Mission was also denied access to locations in the southern areas of the Donetsk region.

The SMM’s remote observation capabilities were also impeded.

On two separate occasions, on 26 and 30 August in Yasynuvata, armed persons prevented the SMM from conducting an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) flight over the Donetsk Filtration Station, threatening to shoot down the UAV and citing orders from superiors.

Aerial imagery on 20 August revealed the extension of a trench line south-south-east of the Donetsk Filtration Station.
