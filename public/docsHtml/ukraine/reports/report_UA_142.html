LATEST NEWS 

The OSCE Special Monitoring Mission to Ukraine (SMM) observed a high level of ceasefire violations in Donetsk region with violence concentrated around the usual hotspots:

the Avdiivka-Yasynuvata-Donetsk airport triangle;

the western and northern outskirts of Horlivka;

areas around Shyrokyne and Kominternove;

and the Svitlodarsk-Debaltseve road.

 Since 9 July the SMM have seen a sharp increase of ceasefire violations in Luhansk region.

A major rise in violence was noted in Stanytsia Luhanska – at the only authorized route for crossing the contact line in the entire Luhansk region.

Violence also erupted around Shchastia where significant clashes were recorded on the night of 16-17-July.

 The SMM observed numerous weapons missing from storage sites and the presence of weapons in violation of withdrawal lines, quite often in or near residential areas.

 Freedom of movement of SMM monitors continued to be restricted, particularly in areas not controlled by the Government.

The restrictions included:

denial of access, jamming of SMM Unmanned Aerial Vehicles, a lack of security guarantees from the sides, and direct threats to monitors’ security through the positioning and firing of weapons close to an SMM forward patrol base.

 On 12 July OSCE SMM Chief Monitor Ertugrul Apakan called for a full and sustainable ceasefire.
