Latest News/Facts Matter 

The SMM continues to increase its presence both in terms of monitors on the ground and in a wider geographical scope.

The OSCE SMM Principal Deputy Chief Monitor Alexander Hug opened another three forward patrol bases Krasnoarmiisk and Svitlodarsk in the Donetsk region, and in Stanytsia Luhanska in the Luhansk region.

in 

Multiple ceasefire violations were observed by the SMM in the Donetsk region, in particular in the Horlivka area, and from 20 January onwards, at and around Donetsk airport and in Debaltseve.

Many of the incidents involved proscribed artillery and mortars, underlining that not all weapons of this type have been withdrawn from the contact line.

The SMM noted ongoing tension in several places, in particular in the Pavlopil-Oktiabr-Kominternove triangle where the sides had re-positioned closer towards each other. 

Although the SMM noted numerous training-related ceasefire violations in Luhansk region, the situation there remained relatively calm. 

On 16 January an SMM patrol was exposed to small-arms fire while facilitating the restoration of a gas pipeline near government-controlled Marinka (23km south-west of Donetsk).
