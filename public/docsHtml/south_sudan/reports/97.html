Aim

:

The aim of this report is to provide findings of CTSAMVM investigations into the allegations, attribute responsibility for breaches of the agreements where applicable and to make recommendations. 4.

Methodology:

a. CTSAMVM Sector Il HQ combined with MVT Malakal undertook a Long Duration Patrol (LDP) to Maiwut and Longechuk Counties on 07 — 13 April 2022.

The team further visited Jekou on 20 April, Malual Gahoth on 26 April and Mathiang on 27 April 2022. b. In Maiwut, Pagak and Mathiang, CTSAMVM interviewed the County Commissioner of Maiwut-Hon.

Bang Kawich Deng, “Brig Gen” James Gatwech Reath (the Turu Declaration Chairman), Brig Gen Nhial Nyal Ding- Deputy Cmdr SSPDF Special Ops Bde in Maiwut, Mme Elizabeth Mete Puok- Former SPLM/A-IO Women League Representative in Maiwut, Brig Gen Wal Deng Ador-Bde 18 SSPDF Cmdr, Sultan Ali Bello-Chief of Arab nomads in Pagak, Maj Gen James Khor Chuol Giet-SSPDF Allied Forces Comdr, Lok Gatkuoth-Chief Executive Director Pagak.

In Jekou, Malual Gahoth and Mathiang, the team interviewed Maj Gen Pal Gatkuoth-the SPLM/A-IO Deputy Comdr 5Div, Mr Deng Pal Kueth-Payam Administarator Jekow, Mr Dolek Tut Kuach-Comdr White Army, Brig Gen Peter Gatwech Puoch-Area Comdr Malual Gahoth faction, Hon Dak Tut Dei-Commissioner Longechuk County.

c. CTSAMVM was not able to go to Wangkuerbiy, because SPLM/A-IO was not able to provide the coordinates of the exact location of their forces.

However, CTSAMVM was able to engage on phone with the SPLM/A-IO Division Commander at Wangkuerbiy. d. The standard of proof used by CTSAMVM to determine whether there is sufficient evidence to verify that a Party is not in compliance with the provisions of the Agreements is “reasonable grounds to believe that the incident that is the subject of the investigation occurred.”

The Maiwut County Commissioner, “Brig Gen” James Gatwech Reath and all other SSPDF Commander denied CTSAMVM an opportunity to go to Wangkuerbiy claiming that the SPLM/A-IO forces were not there.

However, CTSAMVM engaged on phone the SPLM/A-IO Division Commander and he confirmed that he was at Wangkuerbiy.

The SSPDF Brigade 18 Commander alleged that there were spontaneous attacks on SSPDF soldiers by SPLM/A-IO that killed Pte Marko Okil Salvatore Lomin on 15 Jan 22, Pte Nicola Lado Otongo Micheal on 08 Mar 22, and Sgt Sultan Hassan on 20 Mar 22.

The SPLM/A-IO Division Commander denied the allegation and called it a fabrication. 16.

Regarding the fighting in Longechuk County, the County Commissioner Hon Dak Tut Del (SPLM/A-IO) stated that “on 24 to 25 Mar 22, Longechuk County came under heavy and multiple attacks by the forces of SSPDF Maj.Gen James Khor Chuol Giet under the immediate command of Brig Gen. Mangok Wuor.

On 24 Mar 22, Guol and Kapyomdhuol villages were attacked at 0700hr and Biot and Wechlew Bomas were also attacked at 0530hrs on the same day.

On 25 Mar 22, at 0600hrs

in the morning, Biot and Wechlew Bomas were again attacked and during this incident, an 11-year-old girl and -year-old child were wounded but there were no reported deaths.

The civilians fled the villages and went to the Payam HQ for safety.

The area was occupied by SSPDF forces under Brig Gen. Mangok Wour until they were defeated up to Biot.

Both forces lost many soldiers on that day.

Maj Gen James Khor Chuol Giet forces were defeated through the main road to Maiwut and retreated to Malual Gahoth.

Other forces that ran through the road were rescued by the Maiwut County commissioner's vehicle and the injured were taken by ICRC for treatment’. 17.

The SSPDF Bde 18 Commander, admitted to having attacked SPLM/A-lO barracks at Wangkuerbiy on 05 Jan 22 allegedly to rescue their soldier who had been captured by SPLM/A-IO forces. 18.

There was movement of forces of SSPDF 18 Bde from their HQ in Pagak to attack SPLM/A-IO Barracks at Yambura and Wangkuerbiy on 09 Jan 22 to revenge for their soldiers who were allegedly killed by SPLM/A-IO forces. 19.

Col Deng Riak the Military Intelligence Officer (based in Pagak) 18 Brigade, Division, SSPDF supplied ammunition to the SPLM/A-IO defectors specifically to Brig.

Mangok under Maj Gen James Khor Chuol Giet. 20.

Most of the civilians in the villages of Jekou, Biyien, Rar, Jerusalem, Kuerkem, LolNyang were displaced and crossed the border to Ethiopia territory. 21.

There is no humanitarian assistance in the Jekou and Malual Gahoth.

The Maiwut County Commissioner had earlier on told CTSAMVM that, “access to Jekou was restricted”. Assessment 22.Based on the statements of the various witnesses interviewed, CTSAMVM assesses that:

a. Attack on SPLM/A-IO positions in Maiwut County.

It was confirmed that the SPLM/A-IO positions in Maiwut County at Jekau, Wangkuerbiy, Kuerkeem and LolNyang were attacked and overrun on 20 Mar 22. b. Basing on the statement of “Brig Gen” James Gatwech Ruth, to have got support, the type of weapons used and the presence of the Maiwut County Commissioner together with Maj Gen James Ochan in Jekou Barracks after the fighting, it is assessed that SSPDF appears to have supported the White Army to attack and overrun Jekou. Gi Brig Gen James Gatwech Reath owned up leading civilians to attack Jekou using only AK-47 rifles thus contradicting Brig Gen Wal Deng Ador's version that the civilians attacked Jekou using a variety of heavy weapons including RPGs, PKMs and 12.1 mm.

The two commanders made contradicting versions of the weapons used to attack and overrun Jekou with a view to exonerate SSPDF from direct involvement in the Jekou incident. Cc.

Access.

The Maiwut County Commissioner together with “Brig Gen” James Gatwech deliberately denied CTSAMVM access to both Jekou and Wangkuerbiy. d. Causes of the conflict.

Among other factors, the conflict in Maiwut is assessed to have been caused by the alleged disagreement between the government's County Commissioner and the SPLM/A-IO Commanders plus defections from SPLM/A-IO ranks. f. The County Commissioner of Maiwut (ITGONU) seemed to admit that he knows more about the attack on SPLM/A-IO positions on 20 Mar 22, when he stated that, the events that led to the 20 Mar 22 attacks on the SPLM/A-IO positions started from when he took over office as Commissioner in May 2021 and the SPLM/A-IO refused movement of civilians from Jekau, Turu to go to Maiwut. g. Torture allegations.

The allegations of torture of civilians could not be confirmed because CTSAMVM was unable to meet any civilian victims.

CTSAMVM team was informed that the NGO workers who were tortured had been taken to Ethiopia for treatment. h. The massive displacement of civilians from their homes to cross the boarder to Ethiopia was caused by the fighting in Jekou. Conclusion 23.

It is the conclusion of CTSAMVM that SPLM/A-IO positions in Maiwut County at Jekau, Wangkuerbiy, Kuerkeem and LolNyang were attacked and overrun on 20 Mar 22 by the SSPDF aligned forces of “Brig Gen” James Gatwech, the Turu Declaration Chairman together with the Maiwut County Commissioner and most likely to have been supported by Maj Gen James Ochan Puot and Maj Gen James Khor Chuol Giet, in violation of Article 2.0.4 and 2.1-10.0 of the R-ARCSS and Article 2) (a) and (d) of the ACoH. 24.

The political leadership of Maiwut County is responsible for the humanitarian crisis in the area, in violation of R-ARCSS Articles 2.0.10.0, 2.0.10.0 and :
