Introduction , Background:

a. To fulfil its mandate, CTSAMVM is required to monitor the implementation of the R-ARCSS and where required to visit multiple locations in South Sudan to make assessment and observations.

This includes visits to the formations and units of all Parties to the Agreement, including Cantonment Sites and Training Centres.

b. The Peace Agreements (ACOH of 21 December 2017 and R-ARCSS of 12 September 2018) provide that CTSAMVM and its MVTs have unhindered and unconditional access to any area within the control of any Party to enable CTSAMVM to conduct its monitoring and investigatory activities. 2.

Aim:

The aim of this report is to describe incidents which took place on 11 October 2022 at Wunaliet and to attribute responsibility for violations of the ACOH and R- ARCSS where appropriate. 3.

Methodology:

This report draws upon an incident report submitted by MVT Juba The report was signed by the team who were present and harassed during the incident, The team included National Monitors and International Observers Incident 1.
